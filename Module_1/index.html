Research on Global Inequality

Global inequality is about the differences in living standards, income, and opportunities between people around the world. Some countries have strong economies, good healthcare, and access to education, while others struggle with poverty, limited resources, and unstable conditions. These gaps affect things like life expectancy, job prospects, and even access to basic needs like clean water or housing.
